Content used


The content and research that was used to carry out this project was very educational for me. I got to learn a lot about what goes into being a Software developer, the legislations that are out there which needs to be followed, the skills and methods needed to complete a digital project and the different issues that people may face with websites.


Layout of the website


The aim of the design and layout of my website was to be simple yet attractive and appealing to the eye, alongside being user friendly and easy to navigate, which I feel I have achieved with the choice of image used, the fonts and colours chosen and the choice of buttons I have used to navigate to each page.

If I had more time to play around with the layout of my website I feel like I could have presented more, such as use of Flexbox, use of more div to add more spacing, moving buttons around etc, however overall I am very happy with the final product of my website.
